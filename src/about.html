Research Interests

Broadly speaking, I am interested in how people access and contribute to information online. I spend a lot of time thinking about how to make software development more inclusive and the trade-offs between data sovereignty and data accessibility. In the context of my current research, this means thinking about how to design environmental mapping applications that are easy to use and which encourage self-representation while generating, storing, and visualizing data.

I am also very interested in:

  • free and open-source software development
  • design justice and value sensitive design
  • participatory knowledge systems
  • science and technology studies
  • technology, privacy, and computer security
